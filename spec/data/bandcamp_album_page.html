about

Fifty years ago, in September 1965, Otis Redding released his 3rd album: Otis Blue / Otis Redding Sings Soul. Only two short years later at the young age of 26, Otis Redding died in a plane crash. He released six studio albums over the span of four years leaving behind a legacy.

We asked some friends to join us in honoring Otis by celebrating the 50th anniversary of this beautiful and seminal album. For this session, every artist was required to sample any sounds from this album.
